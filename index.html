海华AI

一个挺突然的比赛，让我接触到了一个从来没触碰过的领域，人工智能（AI）。刚开始接触这个东西还挺忐忑的，我以为是要我们自己在短时间内学懂网络神经元并自己编程实现垃圾分类。 学完第一节课我便知道这不太现实，光是概念就已经让我晕头转向更别说是其中复杂的数学公式了。讲真那个时候后我对我需要干什么挺懵逼的。 接下来的课我们又学了梯度，卷积。。。十分新鲜的概念，虽费了些时间可也总算搞明白了。

​ 直到所有课程结束，真正自己操作时我才大概明白了自己要干嘛——训练模型优化结果。简单点来说有一份文件有有关垃圾的图片和所属类别的文件，一个模型通过读取学习这些内容，当他遇到一个从没见过的图片时它可以通过概率计算找到最有可能的结果及垃圾类别。而我们要做的仅仅是让电脑准确的找到文件路径正确读取和改变某些参数让结果准确率更高。绝大部分或者说基本80%以上的工作都是电脑完成的。

​ 这次比赛真的让我学到什么吗，也许从专业上来讲并不多，但这个比赛确实让我深刻体会到了AI的意义，它不再是一个虚无缥缈的一个概念，而是一个实实际际的可以在生活中运用的工具。小批量数据也许人类并不比机器差相比于机器，因为人也许更容易学习某些概念，然而如果这个数量级持续拉大变成了100，1000甚至是10000， 人光是要看就要很长时间更不要说分辨了，这时AI的优势就体现出来了，经过足够数量级的训练学习AI便可以在短时间内经行大批量规模操作，大大提高了效率并降低了出错率。并且比起人工，他们可以夜以继日的不断运行，只要你给他提供电能。 就比如这次我用的是CPU 的transerflow虽然运行时间长跑一次要十个多小时，但我完全可以让他在夜里及我睡觉的时候运行早上起来经行确认和进一步调整就可以。

​ 当机器拥有了学习能力，人只要给他们足够的样本正确的程序，他们便可以自我学习不断精进，且不说更大的，就单单是垃圾分类，未来人们再也不要记忆繁琐的分类类别，上传一张图片便会有程序告诉你他们所属类别，这对人们的的生活会带来大大的便利。

My greatest failure experience

· Arguably, my greatest failure to date has been my competence in English which has led to disappointing results. In fact, I’ve never been brilliant at English but it did not bother me very much until I went to High School. Base on the learning condition and my personal character decided to pursue further studies in international high school. Although I was very keen to study at an International High School, I didn’t realize the importance of having a high level of English competency.

· In the first year,I had significant difficulties in adapting to studying here because all the things I never done it before like new class, new classmates, new subjects and so on. Also my level of English proved to be a stumbling block for me; I thought I had improved a lot but reality dealt me a harsh blow.I got a ‘C’ grade in English as the second language of the final IGCSE examination which means I would have a make-up examination in the September.

· As a result, in that summer holiday I really prepared for a long time. at the same time I also did a lot of preparation which included IELTs exercises. To my own mind my English have improved and I had the ability to pass the exam. As a matter of fact, during the test I did not meet any accident, but this is also the scariest part of the English test and you never know how well you’ve done until the grades come out.

· When the results came out the following January, I was so surprised because my score was even worse than before. This hit my hard and even now I could not understand the reason why although I had to because it was my greatest failure so far in my life. The first thought was that I had been unprepared but this was not accurate – on the contrary, I had made a lot of effort but still could not get the resulted I wanted. In the past if I’d failed at something, I would just work harder but this hadn’t happened in this case and I was doubting my abilities to succeed. This self-doubt was fatal because it left me with no confidence and no motivation to learn. Secondly, this was denial of my efforts which is just like a warning sign that all my efforts had been in vain.

Therefore although I know that it is necessary for me to take this test again I still do not know how to face it. Despite many doubts, I have decided to confront my fears and re-sit the exam. Whatever the result, at least that I have tried my best. I do not want English to become a stumbling block on my way to High School because I understand that life has many stumbling blocks which must be overcome. In this respect, I will not and cannot throw in the towel.
